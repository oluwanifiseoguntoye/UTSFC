STUDENTS FOR CHOICE

ABOUT
US

The University of Toronto Students for Choice Organization is a group dedicated to advocating for abortion rights- an important issue that is often overlooked and avoided. We strive to further feminist dialogue at UofT and promote student wellness against harmful anti-choice groups who invade our campus.

We believe that every individual deserved the basic human right to bodily autonomy, expecially the choice to wether or not to have children. The Students for Choice organization will always make an impact in our school and community.
